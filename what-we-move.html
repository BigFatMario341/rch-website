What We Move

We specialize in the safe and reliable transportation of all types of passenger vehicles across New England. Whether you need help with a standard sedan, a large SUV, a family minivan, or a modern electric vehicle, our experienced team uses the proper equipment and techniques to ensure your car arrives at its destination without a scratch. From everyday commuters to luxury models, we handle them all with the care and professionalism you can count on.

Our services also extend to commercial trucks of various sizes and purposes. We regularly transport box trucks, delivery vans, semi-trucks, and other fleet vehicles for businesses throughout the region. With our heavy-duty towing equipment and trained drivers, we make sure your commercial vehicles are moved efficiently and securely, whether you're relocating a single truck or managing a larger fleet operation that requires multiple units transported at once.

Motorcycles and scooters require special attention during transport, and that's exactly what we provide. Using specialized motorcycle towing equipment, secure tie-down systems, and experienced handlers who understand the unique needs of two-wheeled vehicles, we ensure your bike is protected from damage throughout the entire journey. Whether it's a sport bike, cruiser, touring motorcycle, or scooter, we treat every ride with the respect it deserves.

When it comes to heavy construction equipment, we have the expertise and machinery needed to move even the largest and most complex pieces. Excavators, bulldozers, loaders, backhoes, and other heavy machinery are all part of our regular workload. Our team carefully plans each move, using the right trailers, rigging, and securing methods to safely transport your valuable equipment to job sites across Massachusetts, New Hampshire, and the rest of New England.

Trailers and campers of all kinds are another important part of what we move every day. From small utility trailers and boat trailers to large RVs, travel trailers, and fifth-wheel campers, we have the right vehicles and knowledge to handle them safely. We understand the specific challenges that come with towing recreational vehicles and take extra precautions to make sure everything stays stable and secure during transport, no matter the distance.

Boats and marine equipment also receive expert handling from our crew. We transport powerboats, sailboats, jet skis, pontoon boats, and their associated trailers with confidence. Using specialized marine transport equipment and techniques, we ensure your watercraft is properly supported and protected throughout the move. Whether you're moving a boat to a new marina, taking it in for service, or relocating it for the season, you can trust RCH Transportation to get the job done right.

No matter what type of vehicle or equipment you need moved, RCH Transportation brings over 20 years of experience, modern equipment, and a commitment to doing the job safely and professionally. We cover all of New England and are ready to assist with both emergency tows and planned transportation needs.
